Welcome to Our School!

Colegio de Montalban is a public higher education institution located in Rodriguez, Rizal. The school aims to provide quality and affordable education to students in the community.

Discover More

Welcome CDMians, Students & Visitors

🎓 A Place of Learning Excellence

The institution offers different academic programs in teacher education, computer studies, business and entrepreneurship to help students prepare for their future careers.

📚 Quality Education

CdM offer comprehensive academic programs designed to meet global standards. The curriculum is regularly updated to incorporate the latest educational trends and industry requirements.

🏆 Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we focus on developing well-rounded individuals through sports, arts, leadership programs, and community engagement activities.

Colegio de Montalban aims to produce a graduate who:

⭐ Epitomizes high standard of learning;

⭐ Perpetuates conduct that is rightful and just; and

⭐ Demonstrates exemplary performance in the chosen field.
